# Men's Health in Houston: Checkups and Prevention in Spanish

Hispanic men in Houston often put off seeing a doctor until symptoms become impossible to ignore. At Clínica Hispana Cruz we want to change that: most conditions affecting men's health can be prevented or successfully treated when caught early.

## Why Should Men Get Regular Checkups?

The statistics are clear: men visit the doctor far less often than women, and that has consequences. Regular checkups help:

- Detect high blood pressure before it damages the heart
- Identify diabetes or prediabetes in early stages
- Evaluate cholesterol and cardiovascular risk
- Catch prostate problems early
- Identify hormone imbalances
- Prevent complications from silent diseases

Many of these conditions produce no symptoms for years. By the time warning signs appear, the damage may already be advanced.

## Our Men's Health Services

### General Medical Checkup
A complete physical exam that includes:

- Blood pressure, weight, and vital signs review
- Heart and lung evaluation
- Medical history and risk factor review

### Laboratory Tests
Blood work reveals what a physical exam cannot see:

- Glucose and hemoglobin A1c (diabetes)
- Complete cholesterol panel
- Kidney and liver function
- Complete blood count

### Prostate Health
Starting at ages 40-50, the prostate requires special attention:

- Prostate-specific antigen (PSA) test
- Evaluation of urinary symptoms
- Guidance on risk factors
- Referral to a urologist if needed

### Hormone Health and Testosterone
Low testosterone is more common than people think and can cause:

- Constant fatigue and lack of energy
- Decreased sex drive
- Loss of muscle mass
- Mood changes and irritability
- Increased belly fat

With a blood test we can evaluate your hormone levels and guide you through treatment options.

## Recommended Screenings by Age

| Age | Recommended Screenings |
|-----|------------------------|
| 18-39 | Blood pressure yearly, cholesterol every 4-6 years |
| 40-49 | Regular glucose and cholesterol, discuss PSA |
| 50-64 | PSA, colon cancer screening, annual checkup |
| 65+ | Full annual checkup, bone density, cardiovascular evaluation |

## Warning Signs You Should Not Ignore

Make an appointment if you experience:

- Chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty urinating or changes in urine flow
- Blood in urine or stool
- Extreme unexplained fatigue
- Unintentional weight loss
- Frequent headaches or blurry vision
- Excessive thirst and frequent urination
- Persistent erection problems

These symptoms can indicate serious but treatable conditions. Ignoring them only reduces your treatment options.

## Everyday Prevention Tips

1. **Move more:** 30 minutes of physical activity a day reduces heart disease risk
2. **Watch your diet:** Fewer processed foods, more fruits, vegetables, and water
3. **Limit alcohol** and avoid tobacco
4. **Sleep 7-8 hours:** Poor sleep affects hormones and the heart
5. **Manage stress:** Chronic stress raises blood pressure
6. **Don't wait until you feel sick:** An annual checkup can save your life
